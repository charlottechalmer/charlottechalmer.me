About Me

Hi! I'm Charlotte. I love building things and meeting new people. I have found a passion in web development and am exited to see where this path is going to take me.

After graduating from a small liberal arts school with a bachelor's degree in psychology, I decided to attend CodeCraft School of Technology, a web-development bootcamp that focuses on the MEAN stack. I have graduated with a professional certification in full-stack web development.

While attending CodeCraft, I found that I am perfectly suited for web-development. Although two very different areas of study, it has been extremely rewarding to find the interconnectedness between web development and psychology in my side projects. I used this connection to build my CodeCraft final app Poll Arise. I have also always had an aptitude toward learning new information and excel under pressure, two qualities that have greatly benefited me in my pursuit of becoming a web-developer.

In addition to writing code in my spare time, I love finding good music to share with others, reading (especially satirical novels), cooking / baking, crafting and petting pretty much any dog I see.

Current- Hackathon Project

A four day hackathon project through CodeCraft. combining psychology and web development. We decided to build a program that tracks productivity based on the psychological concept of "Flow" using a timer similar to a pomodoro timer that prompts a user to take a break. The user is then prompted with a pre and post-break survey to get data on their productivity. Technologies used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript/jQuery, Node.js/Express & Mongoose/MongoDB

Poll Arise

My final project while at CodeCraft that utlizes the intersection between psychology and web development-- It is a twitter scraper that utilizes a NLP API and IBM Watson service to run a personality analysis on a user and then outputs which politicians the user is most similar to and most different from. Technologies used: Pug(Jade), HTML, CSS, JavaScript/jQuery, Node.js, Mongoose/MongoDB, IMB Watson Personality Insights, Twitter API, Angular2 templating to retrieve user tweets.
